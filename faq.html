Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who was this school intended for?

The summer school was targeted at both people without previous knowledge in Machine Learning, and those wishing to broaden their expertise in this area. Most of the talks had some technical contents.

Are video recordings of the lectures available?

Yes, the lecture recordings are currently being processed and will be available in the Course Content section soon.

What were the prices?

Information regarding costs and registration can be found in the Registration section.

Was there an acceptance policy to the summer school?

There was no 'application' process as such. Participation was guaranteed upon registration.
