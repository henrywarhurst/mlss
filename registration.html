Registration

The Summer School was not a commercial activity and all the money collected from registrations and sponsorships was used for the expenses of the school. For all attendees registration included admission to all MLSS 2015 sessions, coffee breaks, 1 banquet, and lunches for the working days. The registration fee did not include dinners, breakfasts or accommodation. All prices are in Australian dollars (AUD) and dates in Sydney Time, i.e. Australian Eastern Daylight Time (AEDT).

Early Bird Registration (Until January 15th, 2015)

  • Students: AUD $500
  • Academics: AUD $800
  • Industry: AUD $1200

Late Registration (From January 16th, 2015 to January 31st, 2015)

  • Students: AUD $600
  • Academics: AUD $1000
  • Industry: AUD $1500

Cancellation Policy

A cancellation fee of AUD $50 was charged for cancellation before January 16th. From January 16th to February 10th the cancellation fee was AUD $200. No refund was provided for cancellation after February 10th. Registration cancellations were not accepted unless made in writing to the event organizer.
